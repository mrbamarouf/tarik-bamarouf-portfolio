# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Founders, premium brands, and creative decision-makers evaluating an independent digital studio for website design, UX/UI, brand identity direction, and front-end craftsmanship. Visitors need to understand the studio's taste, capability, and project quality quickly in either English or Arabic.

## Product Purpose

Present Tarik Bamarouf as an independent digital studio through a cinematic portfolio of selected work, process, services, and direct contact paths. Success means a qualified client can recognize the studio's visual standard, understand the offer, inspect relevant work, and begin a conversation with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, precise, and self-possessed. The experience should feel crafted and atmospheric while remaining clear, human, and restrained.

## Anti-references

Generic agency templates, corporate SaaS interfaces, bright or saturated gold-on-black styling, decorative effects that compete with project imagery, mirrored Arabic layouts, and language switches that alter the visual composition.

## Design Principles

- Let project imagery and authored composition demonstrate quality before explanatory copy.
- Preserve the same visual identity, hierarchy, and geometry across English and Arabic.
- Use dark space, warm bronze accents, and motion with deliberate restraint.
- Keep every responsive version intentional while preserving the approved content and assets.
- Prefer lightweight, durable implementation over effects that compromise loading or scrolling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast, semantic navigation, keyboard access, natural RTL text shaping, responsive behavior without horizontal overflow, and reduced-motion alternatives for ambient and interactive animation.
